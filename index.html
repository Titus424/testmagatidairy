Magati Dairy Co-operative Society Ltd

Magati Dairy Ltd specializes in the collection of milk from local farmers and selling it to retailers. We are dedicated to providing high-quality dairy products to our customers while supporting local farmers and communities.The society has over 4,200 registered members.

HISTORY

Magati Dairy Co-Operative Society Ltd is located in Meru County, Imenti Central Sub County and about 15KM from Meru town.The society was registered on 19th February 1984 with 15 members producing 40 litres per day. The Co-operative has been growing gradually by increasing membership and milk production. Its affliated to Meru Dairy Co-operative Union where all the milk is taken for processing.

Services

Magati is a Dairy Co-operative that provides Agrovet services to the farmers whereby it ensures that they are of high quality.

TRAININGS

The Co-operative society offers trainings on animal health, planting and preservation of the fodder crops, breeding, pest and diseases control,treatment and calf rearing.

LINKS TO FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

The Co-operative society links the farmers to Saccos and Banks for easy accces of Loans to improve their dairy farming and for personal development.

CHECKOFF

The society provides Animal feeds, mineral salt, Fodder and AI services on Checkoff

DEPARTMENTS IN MAGATI DAIRY CO-OPERATIVE LTD

Transport

The society offers transport of the farmers milk by collescting milk from all the stations by use of society Track and Motorbikes

Officials

The co-operative consist of 10 board members. Where 7 members are board of management and 3 members Supervisory committee members.

Graders

Graders are involved in testing and recording of milk in the field before its delivered to the society cooler. The society has 20 graders.

Extension Officer

The main duties of extension officer is organizing,designing and participating in training of farmers to ensure increased production per cow per day.

Cooler Attendatant

The cooler Attendatant is responsible for the chilling of the milk once delivered to the society, cleaning of the cooler machine and the room and responsible of a milk weighing as well as selling.

Quality Control Technician

Is responsible of milk quality where it is tested before it put in the cooler for chilling.
